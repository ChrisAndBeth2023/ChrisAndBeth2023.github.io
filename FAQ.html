Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What time should I arrive at the ceremony?

We ask you to be early! While the official ceremony start time will be listed on the invitations for 3pm, we recommend you show up at least 30 to 15 minutes beforehand to have enough time to park and grab a water and fan before finding a seat.

What is the dress code?

Please dress in Sunday best attire! Some APPROPRIATE OUTFITS would be collared shirts, button ups, khakis, dress pants, buttoned up flannels, casual pants, nice dress shorts. Jeans that are not blue are ok (ex. black, brown, grey). As well as casual dresses (NO solid white/ NO mainly white dresses please), maxi or midi dresses, blouses, dress slacks, jumpsuits, rompers.

Shoes can be sneakers like converse or vans, dress shoes, heels, wedges, boots, flats, sandals.

Please DO NOT wear blue jeans, sweatpants, basketball shorts, cargo pants, tee shirts. Please no all white outfits and no dresses with mainly white backgrounds. No rubber flip flops or crocs for footwear. It is not necessary to wear anything too formal like tuxes, neckties, bowties, floor length gowns as an example as the setting will be in a barn. Any questions regarding an outfit feel free to message either Chris or Beth please!

What type of food and drink will be served at cocktail hour and the reception?

Tons of delicious food! We will be having an ice cream social following the ceremony for our “cocktail hour”. We will have a selection of different ice cream in our cart near the venue. We will also have several nonalcoholic drinks to choose from including soda, diet sodas, water, and lemonade. We will be serving a pizza buffet with several of our favorites! We will also have wings. Banana pudding will be our dessert for the evening.

Will you be serving alcohol?

No we will NOT be allowing alcohol Due to the policy of the venue not allowing alcohol to be brought or served without a licensed bartender and the extensive costs of insuring this, we will not allow alcohol at the wedding. *This includes ALL Alcohol, liquors, spirits, etc.*

Will the ceremony, “cocktail hour”, and reception take place indoors or outdoors?

Depends on the weather! We will be holding our ceremony outside the venue at the gazebo. We will be providing a cooler with water as well as paper fan programs. If you need a break from the heat the barn will have A/C you can step in for a bit before the ceremony starts. We will ask all guests to take their seats at least 15 minutes before we start though.

Our ice cream social hour will be outdoors near the ice cream cart. Feel free to find your table on the seating chart and make your way inside around this time and sign in!

The reception will be indoors completely. This will all be weather permitting. If it happens to rain that day, we will adjust to an all indoor event if need be.

What will the weather be like?

Hot like our love! This time of year in North Carolina it will be hot. Please dress accordingly. I would suggest not to wear dark colors since we will be outside for a portion. The barn does have A/C if any of our guests need to cool down. Sometimes rain can also happen around this time of year. If there’s rain, and if it doesn’t last long, we will go on with the show just at adjusted times. If it does last a while, we will adjust to an indoor event.

What will your wedding day timeline look like?

Here are the details! The venue will be open to guests by 2pm. The ceremony will not start til 3pm.

Here is a rough order of events with timestamps:

  1. Arriving, Parking, Finding your seat for ceremony at least 30-15 minutes beforehand
  2. Ceremony starts (will take roughly 30 minutes)
  3. Ice cream social starts / Family photos
  4. Food arrives and Reception will soon follow
  5. Dinner starts and Toasts
  6. First Dance
  7. Mother + Son Dance
  8. Bouquet Announcement
  9. Everyone Dance
  10. Last Dance/Goodbyes (9:45pm)
  11. Clean Up (around 10pm)
Is there parking available at the wedding venue?

Of course there is! There is plenty of parking right next to the building in a gravel lot.

Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

NO.. Just kidding! YES! Yes please! We are encouraging our guests to take pictures throughout the day. Please just don’t get in the way of anyone by doing so. We also ask that you silence your phones before the ceremony as we will be recording it for anyone who cannot attend. We will have a QR code sign at the venue to upload all your pictures on our Day Of Capsule page on our website. You can also upload them directly on our website afterward from home as well.

What’s the best way to RSVP?

We got a page for everything! We will have a RSVP link underneath the invitations on our invitations page. Please click the link and fill out the form with your details and if you will be attending. If you are a couple, you will put down your significant other as your plus one if they will be attending. Any single guest is welcome to bring one other guest with them as well. Please list their name when filling out the form if so.

What is the RSVP deadline?

Write this down! We will be asking everyone to RSVP by June 28th to accommodate appropriate calculations for all refreshments + food.

Can I bring a gift to the wedding?

We just want you there, but I still want that toaster James! Your presence is present enough, but if you wish to bring a gift to our wedding, there will be a gift/card table for your convenience.

When will the reception end?

Up till the AM! The official reception before the dancing commences will end roughly around 6:30pm. The dancing will follow for the rest of the evening until the last dance /goodbyes around 10pm before cleaning up. This of course may shift due to any unforeseen events such as weather or vendor late arrivals.

What is the best way to contact you if I have additional questions?

You have our numbers! If anyone has any questions or concerns about anything relating to the wedding day you can contact either Chris or Beth by text/call and we will be happy to answer any questions and add them to the faq’s!
